About OptiSolar

OptiSolar provides solar radiation data visualization for Sweden. Our platform helps identify optimal locations for solar panel installation based on historical radiation data.

The data provided is found on SMHI's solar radiation data extraction tool.

The data available consists of year, month, day, hour and different metrics of radiation from 1999 and onward.

The project was created to support Sweden's transition to renewable energy sources by making solar potential data accessible to everyone.
